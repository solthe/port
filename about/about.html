About me

So nice to meet you! I am Sol from Seoul. I major in information and interaction design, focusing on HCI, building interfaces and enactable systems.

However, I also have experience managing a food truck, being a salesperson in Mexico, and co-founded a skincare brand along the journey. This explains my being in school for 6 years, everyone now kind of wishes me to leave.

As you may have noticed in my homepage and thought what the hell is ths? I like to describe my skillsets as multi-sensorially functioning, as if each of my sensory organ is a representative fo my diverse and distinct interests.

I am especially interested in enacting multi-sensorial integration via prototyping wearables or interfaces that can provide immersive and sensorially diverse experience. I also love doing projects with data, coding with python and html, building prototypes with arduino. I have also been interested in sentiment analysis to appeal to human emotions when desigining such interfaces and not so detached from everyday life actions.
