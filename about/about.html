About Me

So Nice to Meet you!

December, 2021

I am Sol from Seoul. I major in information and interaction design, focusing on affective HCI, novel interfaces and enactable systems.

However, I also have experience managing a food truck, being a salesperson in Mexico, and co-founding a skincare brand along the journey. This explains my being in school for 6 years, everyone now kind of wishes me to leave.

As you may have noticed in my homepage and thought what in the world is this? I like to describe my skillsets as multi-sensorially functioning, as if each of my sensory organ is a representative for my diverse interests that are united under the principle of human-centered design.

I am especially interested in enacting multi-sensorial integration via careful and ethical design research, prototyping wearables or interfaces that can provide immersive and sensorially diverse experience. I also love doing projects with data, coding and analyzing with python, drawing with JavaScript and HTML, and building unconventional prototypes. I am also currently interested in sentiment analysis to appeal to human emotions when desigining such interfaces and not detach from everyday life actions.
