    In Brief

    Observation.
    Stayed at a bus stop for 1.5 hours; observed the behaviors of people, actions they take, interactions they perform and their surroundings.







    Problems.
    Surprisingly, a huge inherent problem exsited in such a daily used place; people didn't know when to hop on the bus and drivers didn't know whom to pick up.






    Insights.
    • Waiting time is essentially endless
    • They Rarely Use their Phones- Heavy Dependency on the monitor
    • Driver Also Needs Cue
    • Stepping Forward is a universal language
    • Arriving time is really the only needed information.
    • Bus monitor has to be the solution.
