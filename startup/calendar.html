    Background.

    Sauce has been around for a while. I thought a sauce bottle captures the culture and the history of that region more than anything else.

    A hot, spicy calendar on the way. Was I hungry making this? Licked off the screen once or twice..

    We take many sauces for granted. Although humans have practiced eating for millions of years, while foods and cuisines may change, the sauces remain. They have been on our table for too long; a country's unique cultural traces and traits are usually imprinted on a sauce. Discover and define what are the emblems and significance of a sauce with the wildly creative visual representations of them throuh animals.
