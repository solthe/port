Saucy Calendar

A Sauce Visualization Project

July, 2020 -October, 2020 Calendar Design

Sauces have been around on our tables for long time. I think a sauce bottle captures the culture and the history of that region more than anything else. I have sought to creatively re-create the iconic sauce bottles using animal emblems.

Calendar Design

We take many sauces for granted. Although humans have practiced eating for millions of years, while foods and cuisines may change, the sauces remain. They have been on our table for too long; a country's unique cultural traces and traits are usually imprinted on a sauce. Discover and define what are the emblems and significance of a sauce with the wildly creative visual representations of them through animals.
