    Launching the brand
    As the retailer name "don't want no trouble", my partner and I had a meeting with over eight skincare brands in Korea, all of which are renowned both in Korea and the US, with our business proposal. Prior to starting the brand, aiming to distribute Korean skincare to entire Mexico, I was a designer and the salesperson in Monterrey. We have managed to obtain the sales rights from all seven brands both in Mexico and some in the US, however, the launch was temmporarily delayed due to import restrictions and the pandemic. Now, I have created the brand identity manual and guidelines as a fun project (it was never to sell alcohol), as we have decided to focus on our own skincare brand rather than to distribute.
